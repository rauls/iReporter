
#  Help Card
# Report: Audio Clips History
# Please see Languages\English\Help Cards\Readme.txt for instructions.

<!--OVERVIEW-->
This report highlights the most popular audio clips being accessed on your site over time. This includes both streamed files and downloaded sound files. Use this information to assess any unusual trends in bandwidth consumption - audio clips are generally quite large in size, and will apply greater pressure on your Internet connection than normal web pages.

Note that for a file to be recognized as an audio clip, its extension (such as .wma) must be specified in <i>Settings > Analysis > Extensions > Audio</i>.

<!--GRAPH-->
<b>Vertical axis:</b> Hits (default sorting). 
Changing the sorting options in <i>Settings > Statistics</i> will alter the vertical axis to the new sort method. This report can be sorted/graphed by hits, bytes or errors.

<b>Horizontal axis:</b> Days.
All days of the report period.

<b>Depth (Z) axis:</b> Audio Clips.
The numbers along this axis correspond to the numbered entries in the table.

<!--TABLE-->
<b>Files:</b>
URL of the audio clip being analyzed.

<b>Hits (%):</b>
Number of hits to the corresponding audio clip. (Percentage as a proportion of hits to all audio clips.)

<b>Bytes (%):</b>
Bytes transferred as a result of hits to the corresponding audio clip. (Percentage as a proportion of bytes transferred for all audio clips.)

<b>Errors:</b>
Errors generated as a result of hits to the corresponding audio clip.
