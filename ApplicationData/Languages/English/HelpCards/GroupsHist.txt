
# Help Card
# Report: Content Groups History
# Please see Languages\English\Help Cards\Readme.txt for instructions.

<!--OVERVIEW-->
This report shows the traffic over time of grouped sets of files and/or pages which share a common characteristic. Examples of these may include "product pages", or "authorized users only" content. Use this information to assess the consistency of traffic for different content groups, and whether or not their performance is in line with business expectations. 

<!--GRAPH-->
<b>Vertical axis:</b> Hits (default sorting). 
Hits to the corresponding content group. Changing the sorting options in <i>Settings > Statistics</i> will alter the vertical axis to the new sort method. This report can be sorted/graphed by hits, bytes, pages or errors.

<b>Horizontal axis:</b> Days.
All days of the report period.

<b>Depth (Z) axis:</b> Content Group Name.
The numbers along this axis correspond to the numbered entries in the table.

<!--TABLE-->
<b>Content Group:</b>
Name assigned to the corresponding content group. 

<b>Hits (%):</b>
The number of hits to files assigned to the corresponding content group. (Percentage as a proportion of hits to all content groups.)

<b>Bytes (%):</b>
Raw bytes transferred as a result of hits to files assigned to the corresponding content group. (Percentage as a proportion of all bytes transferred for files within the corresponding content group.)

<b>Pages:</b>
Number of hits to files within the content group that are regarded as pages. Page types are defined in <i>Settings > Analysis > Extensions > Pages</i>.

<b>Errors:</b>
Number of server errors generated as a result of hits to files within the corresponding content group.
